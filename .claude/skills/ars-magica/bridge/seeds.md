# Seed Sources — Mythic Europe (Ars Magica 5e)   (hook: seeds)
- deck size: 35            # 30–40
- refresh: each bookkeeping
- sources:
  - The covenant's own apparatus near the PC: vis sources & finds, the library (summae/tractatus),
    the Magic aura & any regiones, the talisman / enchanted devices, the covenant's history.
  - The covenant's people: magi (their Virtues/Flaws, sigils, rivalries), companions, grogs & covenfolk,
    apprentices, the autocrat / steward / turb-captain.

    the twelve Houses, rival/neighboring covenants, Wizard's War.
  - Mundane authority: local lord/knight, bishop & clergy, abbot/monastery, town guilds & merchants, regional war.
  - The four Realms as encounter decks: Magical (beasts, spirits, ancient sites), Faerie (courts, bargains,
    whimsy), Infernal (demons, diabolists, temptation), Divine (relics, miracles, heresy-hunts).
  - Random rolls for novelty, on: generators/story_hook.json, generators/realm_encounter.json,
    generators/hermetic_npc.json, generators/houses.json, generators/tribunals.json.
  - The repo corpus as a seed-mine when more depth is wanted (the Tribunal books, Tales of Mythic Europe,
    Hooks, Realms of Power, etc. in reviewed/ and wip/).
