# Theme Weights — Mythic Europe (Ars Magica 5e)   (hook: themes; FIXED for the whole campaign)
# Every adventure rolls its 5 Theme priorities from these weights. Higher = drawn more often.
# Rationale (grounded in Core Rules Ch.15 Stories & Ch.16 Sagas):
#   Mystery 5  — hidden vis sources, lost Hermetic texts/Mysteries, regiones, the uncanny, "find out about it".
#   Social  5  — Tribunal/Order politics, mundane courts, faerie bargains, troupe ties; most stories can't be
#                solved by raw magic, and the Gift makes social play a real obstacle.
#   Personal 4 — soap-opera arcs, quests tied to a character, apprentices, recurring foes, Twilight/Warping.
#   Tension  3 — high stakes (Code, vis, reputation, covenant survival) but the genre softens character-killing failure.
#   Action   2 — lethal combat is infrequent and usually fought by expendable grogs; magic/law/negotiation solve most.
Action: 2
Tension: 3
Mystery: 5
Social: 5
Personal: 4
# Optional fixed First-Priority theme (keeps the exploratory/numinous/investigative spine front-and-center):
first_priority: Mystery
